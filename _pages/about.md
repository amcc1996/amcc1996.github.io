I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechaninal Engineering and Computational Mechanics at the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto in Portugal.
My research interests are computational contact mechanics, nonlinear dynamics and rough contact mechanics.
I conduct my research within the CM2S Research Group (Computational Multiscale Modelling of Solids and Structures), supervised by Prof. Francisco Pires.
